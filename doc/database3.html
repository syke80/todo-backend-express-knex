Database Design

Add projects

It makes sense to group a lot of tasks.

I borrowed the term “project” from Asana - I think of it as grouping tasks around a shared goal (kind of like Epics in Jira).

This could be something like “React 18 upgrade for the Whatever app”, or “Add Google login to the Whatever app” (just to give Jira-style examples), but it could also be a collection of tasks for the HR team, like “Getting ready for the EU pay transparency directive.”
